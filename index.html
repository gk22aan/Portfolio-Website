About Me

As a seasoned data scientist with over 14 years of experience in the software industry. I have worked with Major Multi National Companies in India and Singapore and have honed my skills across diverse domains such as insurance, banking, and retail. Currently, I'm enriching my expertise by pursuing an MSc in Data Science from the University of Hertfordshire, United Kingdom, expected to graduate in May 2024.

With 4+ years of experience as a Senior System Architect in field of Data science, I've contributed significantly to application development, maintenance, and project implementations, particularly in the realm of data science. My proficiency spans data collection, cleaning, analysis, and the application of machine learning and deep learning techniques.

I hold certifications in Fundamentals of Deep Learning from NVIDIA DLI, as well as SCRUM MASTER (SMC) and Lean Six Sigma Yellow Belt, showcasing my commitment to professional development and industry best practices.

Throughout my career, I've excelled in client interactions and collaborated effectively with large technical teams, employing the onsite-offshore model to deliver cost-effective solutions aligned with client requirements. From project inception to implementation, I've been actively involved in every phase, leveraging my expertise in MLOPS and utilizing tools like Cookie cutter, DVC, Dockers, and MLflows for seamless pipeline and experimentation setup.

My prowess extends to developing and deploying machine learning algorithms, both supervised and unsupervised, and leveraging data visualization techniques using tools like Pandas, Matplotlib, and Seaborn. I've also undertaken proof-of-concept projects for deep learning models, employing architectures such as ANN, CNN, RNN, and LSTM.

In addition to my technical skills, I excel in documenting business requirements, translating complex concepts into comprehensive documents including Business Requirement Documents, Epics, User Stories, Use Cases, GUI designs, and Functional Specifications. My dedication to meticulous documentation ensures clarity and transparency throughout project lifecycles.
